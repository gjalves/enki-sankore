Welcome to Enki-Sankoré

This is a new fork of Open-Sankoré, maintained by Enki company, based at Brazil. The objective is to have a product-ready application to use in digital boards, including the Enki board itself.

Open Source

We believe in open source and we will maintain this application as such.
